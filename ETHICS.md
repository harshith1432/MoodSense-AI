## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection

**What We Collect:**
- Text messages you submit for analysis
- Audio files you upload (temporarily)
- Images you upload (temporarily)
- Analysis results (emotion, risk level, suggestions)
- Timestamps and session data

**What We DON'T Collect:**
- Personal identifying information (unless you provide it)
- Location data
- Browsing history
- Third-party data

### Data Storage

**Stored Data:**
- Analysis results (emotion, risk level, confidence scores)
- Timestamps
- Session IDs (for conversation tracking)

**NOT Stored (by default):**
- Original images after analysis
- Audio files after analysis
- Raw webcam frames

- Analysis history: Retained until user deletes

## Bias and Limitations

### Known Limitations:

1. **Cultural Bias**: Models trained primarily on Western emotional expressions
2. **Language Bias**: Best performance on English text; other languages may be less accurate
3. **Context Limitations**: Cannot understand complex social contexts or sarcasm perfectly
4. **Individual Differences**: People express emotions differently
5. **Dataset Bias**: AI models reflect biases in their training data

### Our Commitment:

- We acknowledge these limitations openly
- We're working to improve diversity in our models
- We encourage user feedback on failures or biases
- We update models regularly to reduce bias
